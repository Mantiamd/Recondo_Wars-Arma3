[h1]DESCRIPTION[/h1]

Recondo Wars is a comprehensive Arma 3 mod designed for SOG Prairie Fire operations. It provides a full suite of Eden Editor modules for mission makers to create immersive reconnaissance and special operations missions — from HVT hunts and hostage rescues to radio jamming, recon photography, and intel collection.

[i]Designed for use with CBA, ACE3, and SOG Prairie Fire. All features are configured through Eden Editor modules with detailed tooltips.[/i]

[hr][/hr]

[h1]FEATURES[/h1]

[b]AI Systems[/b]
[list]
[*] [b]AI Tweaks[/b] — Configure AI skill levels, behavior, equipment removal, and mine knowledge per side. Supports multiple instances for different sides.
[*] [b]Player Options[/b] — Configure player-specific settings and restrictions.
[*] [b]Foot Patrols[/b] — Spawn randomized foot patrol groups with configurable routes and classnames.
[*] [b]Path Patrols[/b] — Create patrols that follow specific marker-defined paths.
[*] [b]Add AI Crew[/b] — Dynamically add crew members to player vehicles.
[*] [b]Static Defense Randomized[/b] — Spawn randomized static weapon positions at markers.
[*] [b]Eldest Son[/b] — Sabotaged ammunition system that poisons enemy weapons over time.
[*] [b]Bad Civi[/b] — Concealed-weapon civilians that pull a weapon when a configured side gets close.
[*] [b]Limit Static Weapon Movement[/b] — Restrict ACE carry/drag on static weapons.
[/list]

[b]Mission Objectives[/b]
[list]
[*] [b]Objective Destroy[/b] — Destructible target objectives with 16 built-in compositions (caches and bivouacs) plus custom composition support. Compositions swap to destroyed variants when the target is eliminated.
[*] [b]Objective HVT[/b] — High-Value Target capture/kill objectives with named profiles, custom loadouts, photos, and backgrounds. Includes decoy locations, garrison AI, roving sentries, and optional Bad Civi spawns.
[*] [b]Objective Hostages[/b] — Hostage rescue objectives with named hostage profiles, ACE captive integration, configurable distribution modes, garrison AI, and turn-in mechanics. Includes 7 built-in hostage profiles.
[*] [b]Objective Photographs[/b] — Reconnaissance photography objectives using the SOG Prairie Fire camera. Players must photograph target objects at randomized locations and turn in the photos. Includes 5 default compositions and Intel Board integration.
[*] [b]Objective Jammer[/b] — ACRE radio jamming objectives. When the jammer object is destroyed, jamming stops. Configurable jam radius, strength, and affected side.
[*] [b]Objective Hub & Subs[/b] — Connected hub and sub-site objective systems for complex multi-location missions.
[*] [b]POO Site Hunt[/b] — Randomized Point-of-Origin artillery site hunt with proximity-triggered spawning and persistent destruction tracking.
[/list]

[b]Intel & Reconnaissance[/b]
[list]
[*] [b]Intel System[/b] — Collectible intelligence items from enemies and locations that reveal objective positions.
[*] [b]Intel Items[/b] — Configure custom intel item classnames and drop chances.
[*] [b]Intel Board[/b] — Visual tracking board displaying all collected intelligence, objective status, and reconnaissance photos.
[*] [b]Recon Points[/b] — Award points for reconnaissance activities like spotting enemies and completing objectives.
[*] [b]Sensors[/b] — Deployable ground sensors that detect foot and vehicle traffic with persistent logging.
[*] [b]Wiretap[/b] — Telephone pole wiretapping system for gathering intel.
[*] [b]Soil Sample[/b] — Players collect soil samples near roads via ACE self-interaction using a required item. Samples are turned in at Intel turn-in locations. Supports optional marker-based area restriction with per-location tracking on the Intel Board.
[/list]

[b]Radio & Communications[/b]
[list]
[*] [b]RW Radio[/b] — Radio transmission system with battery management and triangulation mechanics.
[*] [b]Trackers[/b] — Enemy tracker teams with dogs that follow player footprints.
[*] [b]Reinforcement Waves[/b] — Dynamic enemy reinforcement spawning triggered by combat or alerts.
[*] [b]QRF Mounted[/b] — Vehicle-mounted quick reaction force. When the QRF side detects the target side, vehicles spawn at the nearest road, move towards the threat, and cargo passengers dismount at a configurable distance while drivers and gunners stay mounted.
[/list]

[b]Extraction & Movement[/b]
[list]
[*] [b]STABO Extraction[/b] — Helicopter extraction via STABO rig with body and casualty attachment support.
[*] [b]Outpost Teleport[/b] — Base-to-outpost teleportation with optional destroyable outposts and compositions.
[*] [b]Deployable Rally Point[/b] — Player-deployable rally points with ACE interactions.
[*] [b]JIP Area[/b] — Join-In-Progress spawn areas for late-joining players.
[/list]

[b]Environment & Ambiance[/b]
[list]
[*] [b]Weather Control[/b] — Dynamic weather and time-of-day control.
[*] [b]Ambient Sound[/b] — Configurable ambient sound zones for atmosphere.
[*] [b]Civilians Working[/b] — Working civilians in fields and villages.
[*] [b]Civilian Traffic[/b] — Ambient civilian vehicle traffic on roads.
[*] [b]Civilian POL[/b] — Persistent civilian pattern-of-life system.
[*] [b]Custom Site Spawn[/b] — Spawn custom compositions at markers with garrison AI, patrols, and night lighting.
[*] [b]Camps Random[/b] — Randomized camp placement with composition support.
[*] [b]Destroy Powergrid[/b] — Turn off or destroy all lights within a configurable radius via ACE interaction or object destruction.
[*] [b]Hanoi Hannah Loudspeakers[/b] — Spawn propaganda loudspeakers at marker positions with configurable volume, distance, and cooldown. ACE "Rip Out Wires" interaction awards Recon Points. Requires [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3696734884]Hanoi Hannah Loudspeakers Mod[/url].
[*] [b]Village Uprising[/b] — Civilians wander peacefully at village markers until a configured side enters the detection radius. They then rally, arm up, switch sides, and attack. Each village triggers independently.
[/list]

[b]Utility Modules[/b]
[list]
[*] [b]Persistence[/b] — Save and load mission state across sessions with campaign ID support.
[*] [b]Player Persistence[/b] — Save and restore player positions, directions, and full loadouts across sessions. Tracks units by Eden variable name with configurable restore delay.
[*] [b]Vehicle Persistence[/b] — Save and restore vehicle positions across sessions. Destroyed vehicles are removed on load.
[*] [b]Inventory Persistence[/b] — Save and restore full cargo contents of containers and vehicles across sessions.
[*] [b]Intro Screen[/b] — Configurable mission introduction screens.
[*] [b]Terminal[/b] — Admin terminal for mission control, persistence reset, and master debug toggle.
[*] [b]Arsenal Area[/b] — Configurable arsenal access zones.
[*] [b]Disable Rations Area[/b] — Zones where ACE rations are disabled.
[*] [b]Chat Control[/b] — Control chat channel availability.
[*] [b]ACE Spectator Object[/b] — Enter spectator mode from objects.
[*] [b]Convoy System[/b] — Automated convoy spawning and routing.
[*] [b]Performance Monitor[/b] — Mission performance monitoring tools.
[/list]

[h1]SETUP[/h1]

[list]
[*] Subscribe to the mod and enable it in the Arma 3 Launcher along with required dependencies.
[*] Open Eden Editor and place modules from the organized categories: [b]RW - Main[/b], [b]RW - Objectives[/b], [b]RW - Misc[/b], and [b]RW - Tools[/b].
[*] Each module has a distinct icon for quick visual identification.
[*] Configure module attributes in the module's properties — hover over any attribute for a detailed tooltip with examples.
[*] Some modules require synchronization with objects or units (e.g., sync Intel to objective modules).
[*] Enable [b]Master Debug[/b] on the Terminal module to turn on debug logging for all systems at once.
[*] See the README on GitHub for the full Quick Start Guide and detailed documentation.
[/list]
